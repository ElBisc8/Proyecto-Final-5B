About us
We are a group of people who are committed to people's health and their way of eating daily.
Fair Team | Nutrition is made up of autonomous health professionals, specialists and nutrition experts: pharmacists, nutritionists and doctors with experience in the clinical field, in health consulting and in the scientific (clinical research).
Erick Ricardo Hernández Ayala, Certificate in Nutrition from the University of UVAZA, Specialist in Body Biomechanics and Expert in Nutrition, Dietetics, Clinical Nutrition and Web Programming from the University of Colima, leads Fair Team, in coordination with different health professionals and programmers who participate in this project.
We make available our knowledge of health, as professionals that are his, and our experience in nutrition, general, in different areas, such as tourism, and other areas where communities are the protagonists, whether they are healthy people or people who suffer from a disease where food and nutrition are key to their health. We also act at the individual level, giving solutions to specific food and nutrition situations that affect both clients and people of the groups that specifically request or require it. We apply our professionalism to the maintenance and improvement of health.
